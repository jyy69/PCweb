Company Profile

In 2020, Shanghai Wensi Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd. was established in Shanghai. Core technologies such as continuous temperature monitoring and non-contact vital sign monitoring with independent intellectual property rights have achieved numerous leading results in fields such as the Internet of Things, artificial intelligence, smart clothing, and smart discipline inspection. Most of the company's employees come from well-known enterprises or high-end research institutions such as Huawei, Baidu, ZTE, and Tsinghua Flexible Electronic Technology Research Institute. The core members of the team have experience in developing medical grade high-precision and highly reliable products, always developing products that satisfy users with rigorous and meticulous research spirit. In the future, we will focus on our corporate vision of becoming a leader in the precision of life information and strive to become a world-class enterprise.

Intelligent Health Elderly Care

Based on the technology of non-invasive vital sign detection, data is collected through smart home devices such as smart clothing, smart mattresses, smart pressure ulcer seat cushions, smart seats, and smart toilets. The SaaS vital sign comprehensive analysis algorithm engine analyzes the data to achieve real-time monitoring of vital signs such as heart rate, breathing, body temperature, and posture of the elderly, so as to predict abnormal situations and take corresponding measures in a timely manner, realizing intelligent elderly care services. Improve the health management level of the elderly while reducing the burden on nursing staff. It can be applied to different scenarios such as home-based elderly care and institutional elderly care, and has broad market prospects and social significance.

Integrated Intelligent Care

Based on non-invasive and non sensory health monitoring devices (such as smart clothing, smart chairs, smart mattresses, positioning base stations, etc.) and smart care systems, regular collection and early warning of vital signs and behavior data of the caregivers can be carried out without affecting their daily life and relatively concealed. Through data accumulation, further emotional analysis can be carried out, and advanced technologies such as big data, cloud computing, and artificial intelligence can be fully utilized to achieve smart care. The traditional supervision mode of person to person monitoring will be transformed into a mode dominated by smart care devices, reducing the workload of caregivers and improving supervision efficiency.

Intelligent Farming

Using intelligent ear tags for data collection and an intelligent neck ring as a signal relay, indoor and outdoor signals are relayed to the cloud. By using intelligent base stations to locate and track animal objects, real-time activity trajectories are recorded. Use our self-developed intelligent breeding engine to conduct health analysis (basic vital signs, physiological behaviors such as breeding) and daily behavior analysis (regulatory scope, daily activity level, feeding, etc.) on animals. Being able to accurately grasp the growth dynamics and reproductive process of livestock, conduct dynamic monitoring and trend analysis, timely discover problems in breeding and issue warnings, and assist users in achieving efficient management.
